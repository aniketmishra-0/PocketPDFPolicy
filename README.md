# Privacy Policy for Pocket PDF

Effective Date: 31/April/2026

Pocket PDF ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Pocket PDF handles information when you use the Android application.

## 1. Summary

Pocket PDF is designed to work primarily offline on your device. We do not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal data. We do not require account creation, and we do not run ads, analytics, or user tracking inside the app.

## 2. Information We Collect

Pocket PDF does not collect personal information from users.

This means the app does not collect:
- your name
- your email address
- your phone number
- your location
- your contacts
- your messages
- your payment details
- your browsing history
- device-based advertising identifiers
- analytics or usage tracking data

## 3. PDF Files and User Content

Pocket PDF allows you to open, view, edit, compress, reorder, export, and manage PDF files and related content such as images or pages.

All file processing is performed locally on your device.

We do not upload your PDFs, images, edits, links, or exported files to our servers because Pocket PDF does not use a remote server to process your content.

If you choose to:
- open a PDF
- edit a PDF
- add text, images, or links
- compress a file
- export a page
- save an edited copy
- create ZIP/image output

that content stays on your device unless you personally choose to share or export it using your own device tools.

## 4. Permissions and Device Access

Pocket PDF may access files that you explicitly choose through Android's system file picker or sharing system.

The app only accesses documents and media that you intentionally select or open. It does not scan your device storage in the background and does not access unrelated files without your action.

Pocket PDF may also create local files on your device, such as:
- edited PDF copies
- compressed PDF copies
- page image exports
- temporary preview files
- local project or draft data used to restore your editing progress

These files are created only to provide app features and remain on your device.

## 5. Internet and Network Use

Pocket PDF is built to work offline for its main PDF features.

If you add a web link to a PDF and later tap that link, your device may open the link in a browser or another supported app. In that case, the external website or app you open may collect information according to its own privacy policy. Pocket PDF does not control those third-party websites or services.

## 6. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Pocket PDF does not use:
- third-party analytics SDKs
- advertising SDKs
- behavioral tracking
- profiling
- crash-reporting services that send user data to us

We do not track how you use the app.

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data because we do not collect it in the first place.

Your files are not sent to us.

Data may only leave your device if you personally choose to:
- share a file
- open a web link
- use another app through Android's sharing or open-with features

## 8. Data Retention

Pocket PDF does not maintain a remote user database.

Any PDFs, previews, edits, recent files, or draft information used by the app are stored locally on your device and remain there until:
- you delete them
- you uninstall the app
- Android clears app data
- you remove or overwrite files yourself

## 9. Security

Because Pocket PDF processes files locally, your content is not transmitted to our servers.

However, no device or software environment is completely risk-free. You are responsible for protecting access to your own device and any files you store or export from the app.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Pocket PDF does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any users.

Since the app does not collect personal data, it does not intentionally process children's personal information.
